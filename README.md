# Transport Research

Welcome to **Transport Research**, an open-source research organization dedicated to advancing transportation planning, travel behavior analysis, urban analytics, and spatial data science.


## Research Areas

Our work spans multiple domains of transportation and urban planning, including:

- 🚍 Travel behavior and household travel surveys
- 🚆 Public transit planning and operations
- 🚗 Transportation equity and accessibility
- 📊 Spatial statistics and econometrics
- 🗺 GIS and urban spatial analytics
- 🚦 Traffic safety and crash analysis
- 🌎 Sustainable mobility and transportation policy
- 🤖 Machine learning applications in transportation

## Featured Projects

### Household Travel Survey Analysis

Comparative analyses of large-scale household travel surveys across multiple metropolitan regions.

- Bogotá Travel Survey (2005–2024)
- Mexico City Travel Survey (2009–2017)

Topics include:

- travel demand
- mode choice
- trip generation
- mobility equity
- longitudinal trend analysis

### Transit Operations

Research on public transit systems and service planning.

Current repositories include:

- SEPTA Service Cut Analysis
- GTFS Comparison Tools

Applications include:

- accessibility evaluation
- service reliability
- transit network analysis
- schedule comparison

### Traffic Safety

Research using the **Fatality Analysis Reporting System (FARS)** to study roadway safety in the United States.

Topics include:

- fatal crash trends
- metropolitan comparisons
- roadway safety indicators
- reproducible analytical workflows in R

## Repository Principles

We strive to make our research

- Reproducible
- Transparent
- Well documented
- Version controlled
- Open source whenever data licenses permit

Some repositories contain only code because underlying datasets are proprietary, licensed, or subject to data-sharing agreements.
